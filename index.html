About me

  Hello! As you can tell from the massive writing up above, my name is Raymond Patt, or just Ray! I am an undergraduate student at The Ohio State University studying Neuroscience and Linguistics with a focus in cognitive/computational methods. Due to the nature of my coursework, I'll also be getting a minor in computer and information sciences along the way! One of my primary interests is Language; I've been encapsulated by it for years. As a kid I used to spend hours on end looking up languages resources-- for languages I didn't even plan on learning! I loved having the wealth of my knowledge at my disposal, and constantly pushed those around me to learn the language that entranced me that week. (This week I'm interested in Nahuatl !) More specifically, I am interested in how we can use computers, especially Artificial Intelligence, to aid in second language acquisition and testing.
